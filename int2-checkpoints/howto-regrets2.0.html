    Some of my Regrets

    One thing I regret from this project is not adding enough to my art page. If I used my time better I could have done better on my art page and added more complicated coding. Another thing I regret is not using my time wisely enough. I could have worked longer if I weren't distracted by my table mates and if I didn't distract others too. During future projects I will try to use my time better and not procrastinate or spend time focusing on unrelated work.

    Although something positive that my tablemates brought was a periodical mind break. Every once and a while my table mates would start talking and this gave me a mental break from working and I was able to keep my work pace because of this.
