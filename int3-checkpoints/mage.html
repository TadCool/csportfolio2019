Mages

Here are a list of assassins, each with their own unique ability. To learn more Go onto the League of Legends site or click on their names which will direct you there.

There are three types of Mages, there are artillery, battle, and burst mages.


Artillery:

  • Jayce
  • Lux
  • Varus
  • Vel'Koz

  • Xerath
  • Ziggs
  • Zoe

Artillery mages are in charge of long distane barages against their front line. It's their job to disrupt the enemy so that they don't have a chance to engage into your team. These mages have the longest distance abilities but they can be easily killed once you are close.


Battle:

  • Anivia
  • Aurelion Sol
  • Cassiopeia
  • Karthus

  • Malzahar
  • Morgana
  • Rumble
  • Ryze
  • Swain

  • Sylas
  • Taliyah
  • Viktor
  • Vladimir

Battle mages are sometimes tanky in comparison to other mages but there are squishy (that means low health) mages on this list. Their main job is to engage for their team and be the first ones to start the fight. They sometimes dive into the enemy backline or they try to flank them from behind. These mages, while strong, do low damage in comparison to the other mages but have the most croud control.


Burst:

  • Ahri
  • Annie
  • Brand
  • Karma
  • LeBlanc

  • Lissandra
  • Neeko
  • Orianna
  • Syndra

  • Twisted Fate
  • Veigar
  • Zyra

Burst mages are like assassins but are long ranged, they are what most people think of mages in League of Legends. These mages focus on destroying whoever is closest to them, they do a lot of damage in a short period of time, but the problem is that they don't have a lot of mobility and can easily get targetted by assassins.
