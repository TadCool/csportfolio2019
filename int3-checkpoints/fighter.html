Fighters

Fighters are bruisers, they are characters that are flexable with how they can fight. Fighters usually specialize in being frontliners and engaging in fights. Fighters engage for your team and disrupt the enemy, using aoe (area of effect) croud control. They can also do a lot of damage, or they can be an off-tank (they can build tanky and be a tank even if they might not classify as one).

These are the fighters that primarily focus on diving the enemy team and engage for their team. They are the ones who initiate fights which means that they are the characters who start the team fights. They have one of the most important roles in team fighting, if their engage is weak then their team can't follow up and their team ends up dying. But if their engage is perfect then they can catch the enemy off guard and win the fight easily.

They can also build like a bruiser meaning that they can choose to do damage if they wanted too. They are very versatile and can be whatever the team needs them to be. Fighters are usually very team-oriented, that means that the team relies on them and they rely on the team.
