Assassins

Here are a list of assassins, each with their own unique ability. To learn more Go onto the League of Legends site or click on their names which will direct you there.

There are two types of Assassins, there are slayers and skirmishers.


Slayers:

  • Akali
  • Ekko
  • Evelynn
  • Fizz
  • Kassadin

  • Katarina
  • Kayn
  • Kha'Zix
  • Nocturne
  • Pyke

  • Shaco
  • Talon
  • Zed

Slayers are responsible for in-and-out attacks where they quickly enter a fight, assinate an important player, then jump back out.
These types of Assassins are very mobile.


Skirmishers:

  • Fiora
  • Jax
  • Master Yi
  • Riven

  • Tryndamere
  • Yasuo

These assassins, while they can do the same as slayers, their main purpose is to one v one people and win fights. That means that instead of quick in and out, they do single target damage and attack the enemy front liners. Instead of jumping into the back and focusing one person, they usually attack whoever is closest to them.
