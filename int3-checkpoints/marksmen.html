Marksmen

Marksmen have the job of doing the most damage on the team. They have the job of killing the most people they can before the enemy's marksmen kill your team. If you don't do the most damage on your team by the end of the game, then you are doing something wrong. As a marksmen it's your job not to die and let your team protect you, and in return it's your job to kll their entire team.

Marksmen have the role of ADC or Attack Damage Carry, this means that it's their job to carry the team to victory. All the other teamates on your team have the job to protect you, if you fail as an ADC you basically already lost the game unless there is a mage or assassin that can kill their enemy adc. If you can't deal with that pressure or if you just don't want to be the person responsible for your team then marksmen isn't the right role for you.
