Tanks

Tanks are very important to the team, they are in charge of absorbing all/most of the damage that the other team put out. They usually have a lot of resistances and have regeneration that helps them absorb more damage.

Vanguards are usually in charge of engaging for the team just like the diver subclass from the fighter class. However, vanguards have a lot more crowd control and they have larger aoe attacks that don't focus a single person but instead they affect all surrounding enemies. They do very little damage in comparison to the fighters, but they do have a lot more health than the fighter class. Their main job is to just being annoying and in the way. They have certain croud control's like taunting that force the enemy team to attack the tank instead of the other, more vulnerable, teammates.


Wardens:

  • Braum
  • Galio
  • Nunu
  • Poppy

  • Shen
  • Tahm Kench
  • Taric

Wardens, the are very similar to vanguards but their only difference is that while vanguards focus on disrupting the enemy team and making sure they don't attack your team mates, Wardens focus more on protecting your team. Vanguards are offensive tanks and wardens are deffensive tanks, they focus on cc-ing the enemy divers. For example, if there were an assassin that flanked from the backline, its the wardens job to keep an eye out and stop them from being able to kill anyone.
